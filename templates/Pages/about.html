About Our App

RecInScan is a powerful tool that allows users to scan ingredients and receive recommended recipes, specifically focusing on Kapampangan cuisine. Whether you're looking to explore traditional dishes or try something new, our app helps bring the rich flavors of Kapampangan food to your table with just a few taps!

We aim to preserve and promote the culinary heritage of Kapampangan culture through innovative technology. Whether you're an expert chef or a beginner, RecInScan makes it easier for you to connect with authentic recipes that suit your ingredients.
